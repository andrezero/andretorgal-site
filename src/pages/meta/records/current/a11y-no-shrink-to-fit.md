**Note**: This was previously adopted in 2019 for the [previous version of this website](/meta/records/rejected/react-static) and requires reviewing.

## Details

- not using `shrink-to-fit=no` anymore
- only `<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1" />`

## Why

- users can pinch to zoom and/or double tap
