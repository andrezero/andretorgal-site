## A Colaborar

I am looking to collaborate with small and independent organisations with positive social impact missions, especially those working with socially excluded communities and individuals or challenging systems of oppression.

Available for workshops, advisory engagements, and longer-term mentoring work with teams and individuals.

### With leaders and organisations

- Building sustainable roadmaps and keeping them grounded in real progress
- Shaping deliberate technology choices aligned with goals and constraints
- Supporting teams in defining clear, shared ways of working that fit their context
- Consolidating organisational knowledge so it stays current and easy to update
- Making risk and debt visible, understood, and manageable over time
- Offering leadership education across technical and organisational topics

### With technology education programmes

- Designing curriculum and learning materials for new or existing programmes
- Exploring learning approaches that adapt to context, constraints, and individuals
- Supporting teacher onboarding and enablement

### With students and junior engineers

- UX/UI engineering — building design systems, component libraries, and token pipelines
- Advanced frontend — architecture, performance, security, observability, and automation
- Creative coding — 3D and audio manipulation, for fun, art, and education
- Frontend fundamentals — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, Node.js, and frameworks
- Using AI — learning faster and achieving better outcomes (not vibe coding)
